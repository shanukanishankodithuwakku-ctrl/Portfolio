My Story: From Vision to Execution

My journey began in Sri Lanka, an island rich in culture and fast-paced energy. From early on, I was fascinated by how complex systems work—how strategy, technology, and operations interlock to turn ambitious concepts into thriving organizations.

Driven to master these principles, I earned my BA (Hons) in International Business and Finance from the University of the West of Scotland. This foundation sharpened my understanding of global financial markets, cross-border corporate strategy, and quantitative decision-making.

Knowing that true impact requires a global lens, I relocated to Vancouver, Canada, to pursue my MBA in Business Analytics at the New York Institute of Technology (NYIT). Moving across oceans wasn't just a geographic shift; it was a deliberate step toward mastering the intersection of business intelligence, operational workflow design, and data analytics.
